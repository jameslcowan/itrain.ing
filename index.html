What is powerlift.ing?

A free tool to create and share powerlifting programs instantly. No signup, no spreadsheets — just share a link.

Built for athletes, coaches, and training partners who want a simple way to plan training blocks and share them.

Quick Start (30 seconds)
  • Select your mesocycle and week at the top (e.g., "Hypertrophy", Week 1).
  • Add training days with "Add day" and set the day of week.
  • Add exercises and fill in sets, reps, load, %1RM, RPE, and rest.
  • Share your program: Click the share icon → copy link → send to anyone.

Tip: Your program auto-saves to the URL as you type. The link is your program.

Features
  • Multiple mesocycles: Plan your entire training year (hypertrophy → strength → peaking).
  • Track all variables: Sets, reps, load, %1RM, RPE, rest periods, exercise variations.
  • Share instantly: One link contains your entire program — no exports, no files.
  • Works offline: Once loaded, edit without internet. Changes save to URL.
  • 100% free: No account required, no premium upsells, no data collection.
Privacy & Data

Your program is encoded in the URL you share. Nothing is stored on our servers.

Important: Anyone with your link can see your program. Only share with people you trust (coaches, training partners).
