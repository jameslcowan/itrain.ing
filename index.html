How this works

Your program is encoded into the URL as compressed JSON. As you edit, the link updates automatically. Use the link button in the header to copy/share.

Tips
  • Keep variation names short (e.g. “Paused”, “Comp”, “Tempo”).
  • RPE is optional; %1RM and Load can be blank.
