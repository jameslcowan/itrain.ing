How it works

Three steps from blank week to shareable program.

  1. 1

    Build

    Add mesocycles, weeks, and days. Log exercises with sets, reps, load, %1RM, RPE, and rest.

  2. 2

    Auto-save in the URL

    Your program is encoded in the link as you edit — no export button, no file attachments.

  3. 3

    Share

    Copy the link and send it. Anyone who opens it sees your exact program.

FAQ

What is powerlift.ing?

A free powerlifting program builder. Build in the browser and share your program as one link.

Do I need an account?

No account is required to build and share. Optional accounts for a saved library are planned.

Is my program private?

Anyone with your link can open that program. Only share with people you trust.

Will old shared links break?

No. Legacy /p/… and #/p/… links keep working.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes. The editor is designed mobile-first.
