What is this?

powerlift.ing is a quick way to write a powerlifting plan you can actually use in the gym — and share with a coach or training partner.

The best part: when you tap the Share button, your plan is already saved in the link. No accounts. No spreadsheets.

How to use it (30 seconds)
  • Pick your meso + week at the top (example: “Hypertrophy”, Week 2).
  • Add days with “Add day”, then set the day of the week (MON/TUE/etc).
  • Add exercises inside a day with “Add exercise”. Fill in sets, reps, load, %1RM, RPE, and rest as needed.
  • Clear vs Delete: “Clear” wipes a row; “Delete” removes it.
  • Share: tap the Share icon in the header. Send that link — opening it recreates the exact plan.
Why this helps
  • No more “where’s my program?” — your plan lives in one link.
  • Easy edits mid-cycle without breaking your structure.
  • Coach-friendly: paste a link, get feedback, iterate.
Privacy

Your plan is stored in the link you share. Anyone with the link can see it — so only share with people you trust.
